<p style="text-align: justify;">
Almohadin: escape from the basement is an indie fast-paced 2D precision platformer packed with colorful visuals and bullet hell action. Face a story-rich cartoony adventure inspired by difficult retro side scroller arcade games! The Basement holds many mysteries. Almohadin must take risks and uncover them in order to reach the outside world. Join him on his adventure!
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
Story Mode: A bunch of levels await you with progressive difficulty and the narrative of a visual novel that immerses you in the story of Almohadin, a pillow that magically comes to life and decides to escape from the basement.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
Survive and fight! Bosses: these powerful individuals will try to kill you with all their might. Defend yourself! Rescue: hundreds of subjects are imprisoned in the basement. Make a difference and save them! Massacre: annihilate all the monsters that cross your path. Cleanse the infested areas. Restore peace! Mechanics: along the way, you will find many ways to become more powerful: alfajores that enhance your movement, weapons, teleportation pills, cans of beans that help you fly (I won't explain how...), invincibility potions, and much more!
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
Game Modes! In these levels you must win as fast as possible. Compete against other players to see who is the fastest escapist. Some levels are extremely difficult!
</p>
